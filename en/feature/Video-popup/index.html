FAQs

  • What is the video popup feature?

    The video popup feature in Maxthon allows you to pop out videos into a small, resizable window that stays on top of other windows, enabling you to watch while multitasking.

  • How do I use the video popup feature?

    To use the video popup feature, click on the video popup icon that appears on videos. The video will pop out into a separate, resizable window.

  • Can I move and resize the video popup window?

    Yes, you can move and resize the video popup window to fit your needs. It remains on top of other windows for continuous viewing.

  • Does the video popup feature work with all video websites?

    The video popup feature works with most video websites. However, some sites may have restrictions that prevent this functionality.

  • Is the video popup feature available on mobile?

    The video popup feature is primarily available on the desktop version of Maxthon. Mobile users can use the native picture-in-picture functionality of their device.
